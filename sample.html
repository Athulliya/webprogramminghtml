Welcome to Mountzion College

Mount Zion College of Engineering, established in 2001, has been active on academic as well as non-academic front since its inception. The college has carved a niche for itself in diverse domains such as Engineering Technology, Information Technology, Business Management, Aviation Industry etc. The campus, spread over 10 acres provides a panoramic view of the valleys in all its splendor. The nearby towns Kozhenchery, Pathanamthitta and Ranni are each at a distance of 10 kms away.
